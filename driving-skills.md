# Advanced Driving Courses in the Netherlands

Based on my research, here’s a comprehensive overview of advanced driving courses available in the Netherlands for experienced drivers looking to improve their skills beyond basic license requirements.

## Overview

Advanced driving courses in the Netherlands are generally aimed at skills or subjects which are inadequately or not dealt with in the basic driving course programme . These courses focus on vehicle control, accident prevention, defensive driving techniques, and handling emergency situations.

## Major Training Centers

### 1. Rijvaardigheidscentrum Lelystad

**Website:** <https://rijvaardigheidscentrumlelystad.nl/>

This is the former ANWB test and training center, located at Talingweg 120, 8218 NX Lelystad . It’s now operated by Verkeersveiligheid Groep Nederland (VVGN) and is considered one of the most modern driving skills centers in the Netherlands.

**Contact:**

- Phone: 0320 783 001
- Email: info@rclelystad.nl
- Hours: Monday-Sunday 08:00-17:00 (phone: Monday-Friday 09:00-17:00)

**Available Courses:**

**For Individuals:**

- **Advanced Slipcursus (Skid Course)** - 3 hours, €99-129 per person
  - Focuses on vehicle control exercises, steering exercises, safe braking and evasion through an efficient mix of theory and practice 
  - Learn maximum braking system utilization during brake tests
  - Practice emergency stops on wet surfaces
- **Advanced+ Slipcursus** - Includes accident simulator
  - Similar to Advanced but with additional accident simulation scenarios
- **Intensive Rijtraining** - Half-day training
  - Covers slip control, emergency stops on wet road surfaces, controlling a car on icy slopes, and learning the possibilities and limitations of a vehicle 
  - Maximum 4 cars used, participants divided among them
- **Pro* Rijtraining** - Full-day training in your own car
  - Personalized training using your own vehicle
- **Ready2Drive Training** - New offering for new license holders
- **Caravan Training** - Specialized training for towing caravans
  - Learn to maneuver with a caravan or trailer simulation, including taking mountain slopes 
- **Camper Training** - For motorhome drivers

**Pricing:** Starting from €99 for basic courses, up to full-day trainings

### 2. Defensive Driving

**Website:** <https://defensivedriving.nl/>

Located at Waarderweg 19, 2031 BN Haarlem

**Contact:**

- Phone: +31 (0)23 – 369 90 06
- Email: info@defensivedriving.nl

**Training Philosophy:**
Founded in 1998 by Rob Kunst, who learned driving skills while working for the former Porsche group of the Rijkspolitie (national police) . Rob is the only Dutch trainer for the Audi Driving Experience and provides training worldwide.

**Available Courses:**

- **Rijvaardigheidstraining (Driving Skills Training)**
  - Focuses on vehicle control in emergency situations, which is not part of regular driving lessons or the driving exam 
  - Covers incorrect assessment, improper actions, panic reactions, and unfamiliarity with vehicle reactions
  - Held at specialized locations including Eurocircuit in Valkenswaard
- **Openbare Weg Training (Public Road Training)**
  - Suitable for drivers who cover many kilometers and may experience a certain degree of routine in their driving 
  - Goal is to create more awareness in driving
  - Many previously unexpected traffic events become predictable
- **Privé Training (Private Training)**
  - Suitable for groups up to 3 people seeking training with much personal attention 
  - Also suitable for drivers with exclusive cars who want to discover their vehicle’s capabilities

**Training Locations:** Lelystad, Zandvoort, Nürburgring, public roads in NL/BE/DE, on-location options

**Additional Resources:** Free e-book with “12 Tips for a Defensive Driving Style” available on their website

### 3. ANWB Driving School

**Website:** <https://www.anwb.nl/auto/rijbewijs/anwb-driving-school>

**Contact:**

- Phone: 088 - 214 09 00
- Hours: Monday-Friday 08:30-18:00, Saturday 10:00-16:00

With more than 50 locations across the Netherlands, there is always a driving school in your region . ANWB partners with Rijvaardigheidscentrum Lelystad for advanced driving skills training.

**Available Refresher Courses:**

- **RijZekertraining (Drive Confident Training)** - 2.5 hours
  - For drivers who feel insecure about driving in the dark, unfamiliar areas, fast-moving highway traffic, or heavy traffic 
  - Personal intake interview, theory sessions, practical exercises
  - Takes place in an ANWB lesson car
  - Under €100 for the training
- **Opfristraining (Refresher Training)**
  - For drivers who have had their license for years and have developed their own driving style, helping identify areas for improvement to drive safer and more confidently 
  - One-day training
- **Slipcursussen** - Various skid courses through partnership with Rijvaardigheidscentrum Lelystad

## Course Types Summary

Based on the research, advanced driving courses in the Netherlands typically fall into these categories:

### 1. **Skid/Slip Control Courses (Slipcursussen)**

- Include hydronic systems simulating icy roads and icy slopes 
- Learn vehicle control in slippery conditions
- Practice emergency stops and evasion maneuvers
- Duration: 3 hours to full day
- Cost: €99-€199+

### 2. **Defensive Driving Courses**

- Focus on anticipating dangers
- Public road training with awareness focus
- Understanding vehicle capabilities and limitations
- Available both in-person and online

### 3. **Specialized Vehicle Training**

- Caravan/trailer towing
- Camper/motorhome operation
- High-performance vehicle control
- Electric vehicle operation

### 4. **Refresher/Confidence Building**

- For licensed drivers who feel insecure
- Highway driving, night driving, traffic navigation
- Personalized attention to specific concerns

## Key Features Across Providers

**Training Duration:**

- Most are one-day courses, with emphasis placed on vehicle control or accident prevention 
- Options range from 3-hour sessions to full-day intensive programs

**Instructors:**

- Certified, experienced professionals
- Many have backgrounds in racing, police driving, or professional instruction
- TÜV certified instructors available

**Facilities:**

- Professional training circuits with skid pads
- Wet surface simulation systems
- Controlled environment for safe practice
- Options for public road training

**Pricing:**

- Group discounts typically available
- Corporate packages available
